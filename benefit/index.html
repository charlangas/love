Honest Leah


A blushing history.
Benefit Cosmetics takes pride in their sassy history. They will be the first to tell you about their legendary benetint, concocted in 1977 for an exotic dancer who wanted her nipples to look pretty and pink. For the Spring of 2011 launch of their latest product, "They're Real!" Mascara, they needed a low-cost but effective online promotion that made the brand's history of breaking beauty marketing paradigms justice. If memory serves "make us blush" was a key part of the brief.

Want to get something off your chest, doll?
With the launch of "They're Real!" Benefit created Honest Leah, a well-endowed, sensually mysterious character with an air straight out of a Film-Noir movie. POKE gave Honest Leah a home at ifakeditwhen.com where visitors we're presented with a simple page that "covered up" Honest Leah's gifted figure. Were they real? Of course the most curious would want to scroll and find out - and when they did, they didn't see more of Leah, but rather the page revealed how one could confess their own secrets, (along with video or photo proof!).
