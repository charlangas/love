OutspokenIntense.com


Partners in scent
Empowering women through direct sales of their beauty products since 1886, Avon likes to tout itself as the oldest social network on the planet. Avon first joined forces with Fergie, the lead singer of the Black Eyed Peas in 2009 when they launched Fergie's first fragrance ever, Outspoken. Avon and Fergie came together again in 2011 with the arrival of her follow-up perfume, Outspoken Intense.


It's time to own the moment
Embodying Avon's and Fergie's strong spirit of independence, Outspoken Intense needed an online platform where women all over the world could make their voices heard. POKE created OutspokenIntense.com, a soapbox open to anyone and everyone with a microphone and an Internet connection, giving women the world over a place to speak their minds.


Take the mic
Arriving on OutspokenIntense.com, visitors are guided to the Sound Stage, an open "room" on which people from all over the world can meet to talk, sing, open up and get to know each other or simply listen. Who you will encounter on the Sound Stage is always a surprise: a pair of empathetic ears or someone looking to get something off her chest, or even Fergie herself.
