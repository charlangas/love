The Poppy Project


Meet the Poppy Project
To celebrate the first birthday of Poppy in 2010, POKE and Coach, Inc. created The Poppy Project, a socially-fueled online art project that engaged bloggers and blog readers in the youthful and spirited attitude of the new Coach Poppy collection.

What better way to celebrate than with a campaign as sassy and sequined as Coach Poppy herself. Coach invited a select group of fashion-passionate, style-savvy bloggers to be the first to paint the web Poppy by asking them to register their site and install a small piece of code, a "Poppy Seed."


How it worked
Once installed the seed activated by creating a single Poppy Blossom on their site that—among other things—listened to its owner and their network on Twitter. As it was played with, talked to, and treated with care, their Poppy Pattern bloomed.

The more each fan played with their pattern and got their respective network and visitors to do the same, the bigger, more unique, and more interesting the blossom grew. Savvy participants quickly unlocked many secrets of the campaign such as "creative Tweeting" and its direct relationship with their specific blossom.


Follow the Poppy trail
With each interaction, view, Like and Tweet, the art project grew and grew. The small group of trend-spotters from the fashion blogosphere quickly grew into a network of Poppy patterns and sites that blazed across the social web.

Poppy's fans and their personal networks jumped on the Poppy Trail generating more than 20,000,000 earned media interactions, experiences and impressions. As an added perk, participants also enjoyed personal traffic boosts as fans followed the Poppy Trail discovering new fashion inspiration along the way.
