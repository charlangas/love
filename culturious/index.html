The company approached POKE New York looking to expand its family of brands to adapt to a younger generation of travelers.


Getting you on the plane
We came up with culturious.com to take Tauck's unique sensibility and let technology enhance the traveler's experience from the moment they decided to take a trip to the moment they arrived safely back at home. We provided personalized information and travel tips in the weeks before travelers got on the plane, prefacing the trip experience.


Sharing the joy of travel
During and after the trip, travelers were given a digital platform to easily share their experiences with their loved ones back home. Culturious.com served as a blog for the journey, on which users could relate their adventures every step of the way, at the same time generating content for potential travelers to see what a Tauck trip is really like.
