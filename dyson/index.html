About Dyson Air Multiplier


Introducing the world's first bladeless fan
What do you do when a company known for innovation and revolutionary design turns to you to launch their new, first-of-its-kind invention? Already an established name in airflow technology, Dyson engineered the world's first bladeless fan, the Air Multiplier.

Bladeless fans seeking Facebook fans
Several years had passed since Dyson's last product launch, and the world of communications had changed around them. While Dyson was fully capable of handling a traditional media launch, they needed some help navigating the digital space.

Tech innovation meets social
With that in mind, Dyson turned to POKE to architect their first all digital product launch. POKE created a six-month strategy that would successfully generate buzz about the Air Multiplier through a full-scale digital launch and global retail launch. The entire strategy was executed without using any paid media.
