About FCUK YouTique


Play… Pause… Purchase?
Yes. Looking to showcase their upcoming Fall 2010 collection in an innovative way, FCUK tapped POKE to conceive and launch YouTique, the internet's first Video Fashion Boutique. Featuring FCUK stylist Louise Roe, who recommended occasion-specific outfit ideas, YouTique ultimately allowed each viewer to—among all the other traditional YouTube functions—purchase their favorite styles.

Push play to wear
Louise's recommendations were followed by a clip of a model wearing the suggested outfit, which took advantage of YouTube's in-video annotations to provide Buy Now buttons which linked to the item's page on FCUK's online store with that item already in their shopping cart.

Making fashion how-to's easy to find
Anyone looking for fashion tips on YouTube could search for these videos using popular search terms like fashion, how to, sexy dress, etc. All content and language was designed to be wired directly into YouTube's ecosystem, ensuring maximum relevance and minimum barriers to entry.
