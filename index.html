The PUSH Program is a three-month internship opportunity for those who think they have the chops to work at an award-winning digital innovation (non) agency in New York City. During PUSH you’ll work on real-world projects from inception to completion along side some amazing people. You’ll have an opportunity to dive into everything we do at POKE: Brainstorming, concepting, strategizing, UXing, designing, coding, and Egg Party. At the end of PUSH, if you like us, and we like you, we'll figure something out for the long term.
