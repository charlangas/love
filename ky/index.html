K-Y Keep Life Sexy


Sexy is?
K-Y came to POKE New York in 2008 with an irresistible challenge: to launch and promote their new line of lubricants—the first made for men and women—without mentioning sex. We launched keeplifesexy.com and let the brand's fans define sexy. Visitor's responses could be sorted by age and gender, providing an intimate look at different age groups' perceptions of sexy. And because sexy is a very visual experience, visitors could also upload pictures to a special Flickr pool and show the world how to keep life sexy.
