Bored?
What ever happened to the days of sitting on the couch…at 3am…in your underwear…tapping the remote? Next. Next. Next. After midnight, television was the endless stream of filmic oddities seemingly there just to entertain YOU. Channel after channel. Matlock after Ron Popeil after B.A Baracus…

Meet UPL8.tv. Launched in June of 2008 to quench our desire for curious content, UPL8.tv is a place where we bookmark the weirdest time wasters we can find - and share them with the world.

Just hit the space bar! By pressing their spacebar, users can flip through an endless selection of YouTube weirdness. Just like your Dad did. Fuck.
