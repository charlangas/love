About Thinkstock


Thinkstock Inspires
POKE collaborated with Getty Images, one of the leading providers of digital stock photography in the world, to launch its new service: Thinsktock. Launching a new brand with the infrastructure provided by Getty Images presented an exciting opportunity to approach stock photography in an entirely new way and to come closer to the design community. Thinkstock Inspires gave designers a new platform to showcase their work based off of Thinkstock-sourced images.


The Social Media Art Buyer
We offered subscribers the option to submit image queries to our @thinkstock Twitter account, opening a conversation channel with designers that proved to be extremely valuable to the experience. At the same time, by acting as the middleman between photographers and designers and helping negotiate rights, Thinkstock allowed anyone with a camera to become a stock photographer. The Social Media Art Buyer brought a dying agency role into the 21st century.


Thinsktock for Life
Our social media activity brought the @thinkstock account many new Twitter followers. When @ritalin, General Creative Director at MRM Worldwide in Santiago, Chile, became our 1,500th follower and jokingly asked "What do I get, an iPad?" we said yes. And we gave his agency a 1-year subscription to Thinkstock. Needless to say, @ritalin was ecstatic and helped spread the word about Thinkstock and his two prizes with several tweets and blog posts.
